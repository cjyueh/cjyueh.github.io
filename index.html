yoga with christina.

My earliest exposure to yoga was at a summer camp during high school. I had no idea what I was getting into and was incredibly stiff and not at all athletic. Many years later, on a health kick, I resumed my yoga practice in all seriousness. Since then, my practice has evolved into a path of learning about how my body and mind work, a process of creating space within and beyond myself, and an exploration of contrasts.

As a teacher, I aim to guide students on the path of self-discovery and to promote a space where exploration is welcomed. I provide detailed instruction beyond the poses, so students can learn healthy body alignment and actions that will build strength and promote ease within their practice and beyond. I hope to see you on the mat!
